Autistic Words with Jason

I once asked my brother to define a couple words for me. He told me that people are too loose with the definitions of words such as few, many, or now. Unfortunately, I was about to disappoint him with my lack of vocabulary policing. First, I asked him about the duality within life and love. Then, we chatted about why work is often prefaced by “have to.” Our conversation was the product of an autistic genius and a shadow artist. Nonetheless, creativity was born. I’d say it was a success.
